Rock/Alternative

Rock Music is Timeless

They say good music never gets old, and when it comes to rock and metal, that’s proven to be true. Music is art and beautiful art can never be outdated. There are rock songs from The Beatles, Rolling Stones, and AC/DC that will never go out of style and that are still being played heavily today. It’s just something about the way that rock music makes you feel and think. People prefer to listen to classic rock music with real meaning than to listen to the regurgitated lyrics of today’s songs with no real significance. This is why listening to rock songs, whether old or new, never goes out of style - Mauricio Vega

Rap/Hip-Hop

I don't know what I would do without todays modern Rap & Hip-Hop. This is by far my favorite genre of music to listen to. Majority of the songs for this genre contain fast uptempo beats. Personally, I adore this genre more than any other because it fuels myself with energy and confidence. Overall, Rap & Hip-Hop is enjoyable to listen to and it brings folks together in a positive way. -Ishmael Burton

Jazz

Jazz. It's not just coffee shop music that you listen to when you want to be cool, seductive and mysterious. It's music that attempts to get you feeling things through the actual sound of the instruments being played, rather than the lyrics. Most of the time, at least. It's music that's hard to define as being slow or relaxing because as soon as you say that, someone can pull up a track that goes as fast as the musicians can go. Jazz can be universally appreciated thanks to its focus on instruments, and even if there were lyrics of a different language, you can't help but hum or sway to the melody. Jazz music is fantastic music to listen to at any time, and hopefully more of you folks can get to experience it. - Jeremy Jacob Francisco
